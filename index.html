OnePiece

"One Piece" is an epic tale of adventure following a pirate named Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, known as the Straw Hat Pirates. Luffy harbors a grand dream of finding the legendary treasure known as the One Piece and becoming the Pirate King. Alongside his spirited companions, he sails across dangerous seas teeming with pirates, bizarre creatures, and various challenges. Throughout their journey, they encounter a diverse array of characters and confront powerful enemies, including the World Government's Navy and other pirate crews. With his Devil Fruit powers, epic battles, and strong friendships, Luffy and his crew continue to strive towards their dreams while discovering the true meaning of adventure and freedom.

Attack On Titan

"Attack on Titan" is a story set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to terrifying attacks from humanoid creatures known as "Titans." The remnants of humanity live within large fortress cities to protect themselves from the Titan threat. The story follows Eren Yeager, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armin Arlert, three friends determined to eradicate Titans after their city is attacked and Eren's mother is killed by one of the Titans. They join the Scout Regiment to fight against the Titans and uncover the mysteries behind their origins. However, they soon discover that there is a larger conspiracy behind the war against the Titans, and their battle against these creatures reveals dark secrets that shake the foundations of their world.

Koi To Uso

"Love and Lies" is set in a society where the government arranges marriages based on genetic compatibility to ensure social stability. The story revolves around Yukari Nejima, a high school student who harbors feelings for his classmate, Misaki Takasaki. However, before he can confess, he receives his government-assigned marriage partner, Ririna Sanada. As Yukari navigates his complicated feelings and the societal pressures surrounding him, he finds himself entangled in a love triangle that challenges the boundaries of his heart and the rules of the world he lives in.

Koe No Katachi

"A Silent Voice," also known as "Koe no Katachi," is a story about a young boy named Shoya Ishida who bullies a deaf girl named Shoko Nishimiya when they are in elementary school. After being punished and isolated by his own friends, Shoya grows into a teenager filled with regret and loneliness. However, he gets a chance at redemption when he reconnects with Shoko as a teenager. Shoya endeavors to make amends for his past mistakes and form a better relationship with Shoko while confronting his past and new challenges that arise. It's a tale of redemption, friendship, and heartwarming personal growth.

Kimi no Na wa

"Your Name" tells the story of two teenagers, Mitsuha and Taki, who mysteriously begin swapping bodies at random. Mitsuha is a girl from a rural town who yearns for life in the big city, while Taki is a high school student in Tokyo. Through these unexpected body swaps, they start living each other's lives, learning about their differences and similarities. However, when they attempt to meet face-to-face, they discover that their fates are connected by a tragic event that alters everything. With the help of memories and clues from their body swaps, Mitsuha and Taki strive to find a way to save each other and the world around them. "Your Name" is a captivating tale of love, destiny, and the miracles that occur between two souls that are meant to be intertwined.

Suzume no Tojimari

"Suzume no Tojimari" is a tale of a young girl named Suzume, whose life is intertwined with an ancient legend about a river deity. Every spring, Suzume seeks to find this deity, hoping to obtain a miracle that could cure her father's illness. However, in her quest, she discovers something much greater: a dark secret that has been hidden in her village for centuries. With the help of her new friends, Suzume decides to uncover the truth behind the legend, facing dangers and challenges along the way. It is a story of adventure, bravery, and the power of friendship that overcomes all obstacles.

Gintama

"Gintama" is a comedic tale set in an alternative world during the Bakumatsu period in Edo, invaded by aliens known as the Amanto. The story follows the life of a samurai named Gintoki Sakata, who once was a brave warrior but now dedicates himself as a freelancer taking on various odd jobs, ranging from mundane tasks to absurd ones. Alongside his eclectic companions, Shinpachi Shimura and Kagura, they embark on wild adventures filled with comedy, parody, and unexpected action. Amidst the chaos, "Gintama" also delivers moments that are deep and emotional, touching on themes of friendship, courage, and the meaning of life.

Grand Blue

"Grand Blue" is a comedy anime centered around Iori Kitahara, a college student who moves to a coastal town to attend university. Upon arriving, he is drawn into the eccentric world of diving and partying by his uncle and his group of friends at the diving shop, "Grand Blue." The series follows Iori as he navigates the chaotic and alcohol-fueled adventures with his new friends, experiencing outrageous parties, diving mishaps, and hilarious situations. Despite the wild antics, "Grand Blue" also explores themes of friendship, self-discovery, and the pursuit of one's passions. In the third season, the students return to school after summer break, and the new headteacher, Ms. Hope Haddon, introduces strict rules and regulations. The tension between Otis and Maeve continues, and the students witness public humiliation, leading to Hope’s resignation. However, their protest has unintended consequences, jeopardizing the school’s future. Meanwhile, Otis and Maeve finally come together, but Maeve’s acceptance into a program in the United States becomes a hurdle.
